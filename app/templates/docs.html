The Learning Path

Welcome to the Safebox Advisor Platform. We have designed our training resources with a specific linear progression in mind. Unlike a typical help center where you might search for random topics, our video library is structured as a course.

To truly master the platform and provide the highest level of service to your clients, we strongly recommend watching the videos in their numbered order.

Your Journey to Expertise

01

Foundation & Philosophy

The first set of videos explains why Safebox exists and the unique value proposition for high-net-worth families. Understanding this "why" is crucial for your client conversations.

02

Core Functionality

Next, we dive into the "how". These videos cover the essential mechanics: uploading documents, organizing the vault, and setting up permissions. These build directly upon the foundational concepts.

03

Once you grasp the core, we introduce advanced features like legacy handoff protocols and family sharing. These features require a solid understanding of the basics to configure correctly.
